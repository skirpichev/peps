Abstract
========

Introduce new imaginary type and mixed rules for complex arithmetics.


Motivation
==========

Too much special casing, rectangular notation, brain split wrt repr() & str
input, fix eval-repr round-trip, fix simple invariants in docs.  Small
performance boost.

* C standard since C99
* GSL
* MPC has special cases for real operands

`cmath.asin
<https://github.com/python/cpython/blob/918e3ba6c0cbb49034a0b03219a2ddd33cb4498b/Modules/cmathmodule.c#L249-L256>`_
from the stdlib::

.. code:: python

   def asin(z):
       # asin(z) = -i asinh(iz)
       z = complex(-z.imag, z.real)  # z -> iz
       z = cmath.asinh(z)
       return complex(z.imag, -z.real)  # z -> -iz

Examples:

at z = ±2±0j

* atan = lambda z: 1j*(cmath.log(1 - 1j*z) - cmath.log(1 + 1j*z))/2
  - bad imaginary component in both cases
* asin = lambda z: -1j*cmath.asinh(1j*z)

at z = +2±0j

* asin = lambda z: -1j*cmath.log(1j*z + cmath.sqrt(1 - z*z))

at z = -2±0j

* asin = lambda z: 1j*cmath.log(cmath.sqrt(1 - z*z) - 1j*z)


Not a replacement to limit() in CAS!

.. code-block:: pycon

   >>> log1p = lambda z: +z - z*z/2  # ln(1+z) up to O(z^3)
   >>> log1m = lambda z: -z - z*z/2  # ln(1-z) up to O(z^3)
   >>> log1p(0.25-0j)  # wrong sign!
   (0.21875+0j)
   >>> log1p(0.25-1e-10j)
   (0.21875-7.5e-11j)
   >>> log1p(0.25-1e-30j)
   (0.21875-7.500000000000001e-31j)
   >>> log1p(0.25-1e-300j)
   (0.21875-7.5e-301j)
   >>> log1m(0.25-0j)  # correct sign
   (-0.28125+0j)
   >>> log1m(0.25-1e-300j)
   (-0.28125+1.25e-300j)


.. code-block:: pycon

   >>> log1p = lambda z: +z - z*z/2  # ln(1+z) up to O(z^3)
   >>> import cmath
   >>> z = 0.25-0j
   >>> cmath.log(1+z)
   (0.22314355131420976-0j)
   >>> log1p(z)
   (0.21875+0j)
   >>> z = 0.25-0j
   >>> cmath.log(1-z)
   (-0.2876820724517809+0j)
   >>> log1m = lambda z: -z - z*z/2  # ln(1-z) up to O(z^3)
   >>> log1m(z)
   (-0.28125+0j)

Rationale
=========

C17 Annex G

Specification
=============

We need just one pure imaginary constant, i (``1j``).

+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| lhs\/rhs | u          | iv           | u + iv             |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| x        | x ± u      | x ± iv       | (x ± u) ± iv       |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| iy       | ±u + iy    | i(y ± v)     | ±u + i(y ± v)      |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| x + iy   | x ± u + iy | x + i(y ± v) | (x ± u) + i(y ± v) |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+

+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| lhs\/rhs | u              | iv              | u + iv                     |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| x        | x * u          | i(x*v)          | (x*u) + i(x*v)             |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| iy       | i(y*u)         | -y*v            | (-y*v) + i(y*u)            |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| x + iy   | (x*u) + i(y*u) | (-y*v) + i(x*v) | (x*u - y*v) + i(y*u + x*v) |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+

+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| lhs\/rhs | u              | iv              |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| x        | x / u          | i(-x/v)         |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| iy       | i(y/u)         | y/v             |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| x + iy   | (x/u) + i(y/u) | (y/v) + i(-x/v) |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+

Backwards Compatibility
=======================

Low impact for users: sign of zero, meaningless results replaced

1j*1j -> float (not complex)
