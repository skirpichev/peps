Abstract
========

Introduce new imaginary type and mixed rules for complex/imaginary/real
arithmetics.


Motivation
==========

Rectangular notation, brain split wrt repr() & str input, fix eval-repr
round-trip, fix simple invariants in docs.


Rationale
=========

C11 Annex G


Specification
=============

+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| lhs\/rhs |   u        |     iv       |   u + iv           |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| x        | x ± u      |  x ± iv      | (x ± u) ± iv       |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| iy       | ±u + iy    | i(y ± v)     | ±u + i(y ± v)      |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+
| x + iy   | x ± u + iy | x + i(y ± v) | (x ± u) + i(y ± v) |
+----------+------------+--------------+--------------------+

+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| lhs\/rhs |   u            |     iv          |   u + iv                   |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| x        | x * u          |  i(x*v)         | (x*u) + i(x*v)             |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| iy       | i(y*u)         |  -y*v           | (-y*v) + i(y*u)            |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+
| x + iy   | (x*u) + i(y*u) | (-y*v) + i(x*v) | (x*u - y*v) + i(y*u + x*v) |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+----------------------------+

+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| lhs\/rhs |   u            |     iv          |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| x        | x / u          |  i(-x/v)        |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| iy       | i(y/u)         |  y/v            |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
| x + iy   | (x/u) + i(y/u) | (y/v) + i(-x/v) |
+----------+----------------+-----------------+
